About Me

My name is Abel Abate. I was born to Abate Lemu and Etay Abreha on January 11, 2000 in Addis Ababa ,Ethiopia. I started school at the age of 4 at Ethio-Parents' School in Gerji,Addis Ababa. I finished primary and secondary education at this same school. I graduated high school in the year 2017/18 and applied to Addis Ababa University in the field of engineering. I am currently a 1st year student at Addis Ababa University studying Software engineering. I have one older sibling, a sister named Eden Abate who is 3 years older than me. She is currently a 4th year student at SPHMMC. This is my personal website where I plan to write about things that exite me,share what I am learning in this or any other field I gravitate towards and give updates.
