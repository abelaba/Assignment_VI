Mucho player

by Abel Abate on March 10, 2019



Listening to music is a hobby of almost every person you meet around daily, for playing this music we need to have installed a music player on our device. Mucho player is an app developed using python which lets you add music to your library and listen to them. It was built by using the pygame library. Pygame is a library that gives us the power of playing with different multimedia formats like audio, video, etc.
